Wir sind der
Roboterclub Aachen e.V.

Wir bauen autonome Roboter, um am internationalen Eurobot-Wettbewerb teilzunehmen. Dabei verbinden wir die Themenbereiche Maschinenbau, Elektrotechnik und Informatik.

Eurobot Germany 2020

01 - 03 May 2020 in Aachen

The Eurobot 2020 will take place this year under the motto "Sail the World". This year, the Roboterclub Aachen is the organiser of the German competition, which serves the German teams as a qualification for the international competition in France.
