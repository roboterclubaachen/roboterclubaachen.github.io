We are German champion 2016

May 3, 2016

We are qualified for the international contest in France! Our robots, Hassel and Hoff, won every game at the German Eurobot contest 2016 in Stuttgart.

The competition was well organized by VtAS. Thank you for the great time!

We would like to thank our sponsors for their generous support.
