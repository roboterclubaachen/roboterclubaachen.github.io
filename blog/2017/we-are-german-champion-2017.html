We are German champion 2017

May 1, 2017

We are qualified for the international contest in France!

Our robots, Juri and Siggi, won nearly every game at the German Eurobot contest 2018 in Dresden. The competition was well organized by TURAG. Thank you for the great time!

We would like to thank our sponsors and supporters for their generous backing.
