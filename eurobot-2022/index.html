Motto: Age of Bots

Eurobot Germany 2022

The Eurobot 2022 will take place this year under the motto “Age of Bots”.

Eurobot Contest

  • When: Saturday 8th and Sunday 9th October 2022
  • Where: ICT cubes, Kopernikusstraße 16, 52074 Aachen, Germany
  • Who: All German and international Eurobot teams
  • Registration: Please contact us.
  • Entry fee: 100€ per team (please contact us if your team can’t afford that.)
  • Motto: Age of Bots
  • Rules: The rules of Eurobot 2022 apply
  • Audience: Spectators are very welcome

Technical Innovation Contest

Each team presents its most innovative design from the robot in a 15-minute presentation. An independent jury from science and industry evaluates the innovations and awards the Technical Innovation prize to the best innovation and presentation.
